Timeline Editor Guide

How to Use:

  • Click frames to select them
  • Use +/- buttons to adjust hold duration
  • Uncheck "Include" to skip frames
  • Use transport controls to preview

Frame Timing:

  • 1-2 frames: Fast action (punches, swings)
  • 2-3 frames: Normal movement
  • 4-6 frames: Slow, careful motion
  • 6+ frames: Dramatic pauses, reactions

Stop Motion Tips:

  • Fast actions need fewer frames to feel snappy
  • Hold key poses longer for emphasis
  • Vary timing to create natural rhythm
  • Preview often to check feel
